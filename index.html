About Me

Hi, I'm Niragi Kodagoda, a passionate Front-End Developer and UI/UX Designer from Sri Lanka. I'm currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science with Data Science at Coventry University via NIBM. I love crafting clean, intuitive, and engaging digital experiences that put the user first, blending creativity and technology to build meaningful solutions. As an active IEEE student member, I continually seek to grow my skills and stay at the forefront of innovation.

My approach to front-end development goes beyond writing code — I carefully consider layout, typography, colour, and interaction to ensure every interface feels natural and purposeful. I work primarily with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, and use Figma for UI design and prototyping.

Driven by curiosity and creativity, I continuously explore new tools and technologies to enhance my development and design skills. I'm always excited to connect, collaborate, and contribute to projects that make a real impact.
